# WarmPath Quick Start

Get going in under 2 minutes — no terminal, no setup.

---

## Step 1 — Download the app

Download [`index.html`](https://jaganmohankb-git.github.io/warmpath/) from the WarmPath GitHub page, or open it directly in your browser from the hosted version.

---

## Step 2 — Open it in your browser

Double-click `index.html` to open it. That's all — no installation needed.

> **Tip for Mac:** If you see a security warning, right-click the file → Open → Open anyway.

---

## Step 3 — Import your contacts or use Quick Start

**Option A — LinkedIn archive (recommended for your full network):**
1. Go to LinkedIn → Me → Settings & Privacy → Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data
2. Request your full archive (takes 10–30 min to arrive by email)
3. In WarmPath → Setup → Import, drag and drop the `.zip` file

**Option B — Quick Start (get going right now):**
1. In WarmPath → Setup → Import, click **Quick Start**
2. Add up to 10 contacts manually — name, company, role, and how you know them
3. Click **Save & go to my network** — your contacts are scored and ready

---

> **Heads up:** WarmPath stores everything in your browser's local storage. Nothing leaves your device.  
> Use **Setup → Settings → Backup** to save your data as a file.
